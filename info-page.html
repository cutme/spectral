Support contract

Most of our customers have support agreements with us. We believe that this is the best way to make sure your investment will be useable for many years and that downtime is minimized.

We have some standard forms of support contracts listed below, but if you have a different idea on how you want to be supported, please let us know.

Preventive maintenance contract

In the preventive maintenance contract (PM) we cover the necessary support to keep your equipment in shape. Based on the type of system that you have, how it is used and what the consequence would be of possible down time we agree on a suitable frequency and what things should be maintained, cleaned, updated or aligned.

The contract includes all costs for traveling and living expenses related to the planned visits.

Extended support contract

In addition to the maintenance covered in the PM contract you might want to include some additional items, a number of free repair visits, an annual training day, software updates, etc. Add these from a menu of options to create your extended contract.

Full support contract

To provide you with a fixed running costs and an insurance against any unexpected incidents it is possible to sign a full service contract that not only includes preventive maintenance and free repair visits but also free spare parts.

General

With any of the support contracts you have your own service engineer that can be reached with a mobile phone number and email. Your engineer will get to know both you and your instrument, its history and how to manage it in the best way. When needed additional resources can be called in for specific work, but your contact will remain in charge.

If you are located in different country our travel expenses and travel time will never be more than if we would be based in your country capital. For planned preventive maintenance the travel expenses are always included in the PM contract.
